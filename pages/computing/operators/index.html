Let's explore some of the basic operators:</div> <div class="header">1. Arithmetic Operators</div> <div class="text">These operators perform mathematical operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.</div> <div class="sub-header">- Addition (+)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> result = 5 + 3 # result will be 8 </div> <div class="sub-header">- Subtraction (-)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> result = 10 - 4 # result will be 6 </div> <div class="sub-header">- Multiplication (*)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> result = 2 * 3 # result will be 6 </div> <div class="sub-header">- Division (/)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> result = 15 / 3 # result will be 5.0 (float) </div> <div class="header">2. Comparison Operators</div> <div class="text">These operators compare two values and return a Boolean result (True or False).</div> <div class="sub-header">- Equal to (==)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> is_equal = 5 == 5 # is_equal will be True </div> <div class="sub-header">- Not equal to (!=)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> not_equal = 10 != 5 # not_equal will be True </div> <div class="sub-header">- Greater than (>)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> greater_than = 15 > 10 # greater_than will be True </div> <div class="sub-header">- Less than (<)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> less_than = 7 < 9 # less_than will be True </div> <div class="header">3. Logical Operators</div> <div class="text">These operators combine multiple conditions and return a Boolean result.</div> <div class="sub-header">- AND (and)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> both_conditions = (5 > 3) and (10 < 15) # both_conditions will be True </div> <div class="sub-header">- OR (or)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> either_condition = (5 < 3) or (10 < 15) # either_condition will be True </div> <div class="sub-header">- NOT (not)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> not_condition = not (5 > 3) # not_condition will be False </div> <div class="header">4. Assignment Operators</div> <div class="text">These operators assign values to variables.</div> <div class="sub-header">- Assignment (=)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> x = 10 # x is assigned the value 10 </div> <div class="sub-header">- Add and Assign (+=)</div> <div class="text">Example:<br> y = 5<br> y += 3 # y will be 8 </div> <div class="header">Conclusion</div> <div class="text">Understanding operators is crucial for writing effective Python code.
