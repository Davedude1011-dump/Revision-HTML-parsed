Effects of Flooding</div> <div class="text">- **Loss of Life and Property:** Flooding can pose significant threats to human life and property, causing damage to homes, infrastructure, and agricultural land.</div> <div class="text">- **Displacement of Communities:** Floods can force people to evacuate their homes, leading to temporary or permanent displacement.</div> <div class="text">- **Economic Impact:** The destruction of infrastructure, crops, and businesses can have a substantial economic impact on affected regions.</div> <div class="text">- **Water Contamination:** Floodwaters can carry pollutants and contaminants, leading to waterborne diseases and contamination of water sources.</div> <div class="text">- **Erosion and Sedimentation:** Floods can result in soil erosion and the deposition of sediments, affecting the landscape and river ecosystems.</div> <div class="text">- **Impact on Wildlife:** Flooding can disrupt ecosystems, affecting wildlife habitats and leading to the displacement or loss of animal species.</div> <div class="header">3.
