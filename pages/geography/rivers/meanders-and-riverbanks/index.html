Role of River Banks</div> <div class="text">- **Containment of Flow:** River banks serve as natural boundaries that contain the flow of water within the river channel. They prevent lateral spreading and guide the water along a defined course.</div> <div class="text">- **Bank Erosion:** While river banks provide containment, they are also subject to erosion, especially on the outer side of meanders.
