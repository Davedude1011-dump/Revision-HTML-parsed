<div class="subject">Geography</div> <div class="title">Climate Change - Mitigation</div> <br> <div class="date">7/12/23</div> <br><br><br> <div class="header">Mitigation Methods for Climate Change</div> <div class="text">Mitigation involves efforts to reduce or prevent the emission of greenhouse gases, addressing the root causes of climate change. Here are key mitigation methods:</div> <div class="header">1. **Renewable Energy Transition**</div> <div class="text">Shifting from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydropower to decrease carbon emissions from energy production.</div> <div class="header">2. **Energy Efficiency**</div> <div class="text">Implementing energy-efficient technologies and practices in industries, transportation, and buildings to reduce overall energy consumption and emissions.</div> <div class="header">3. **Afforestation and Reforestation**</div> <div class="text">Planting trees and restoring forests to absorb carbon dioxide from the atmosphere, acting as a natural carbon sink.</div> <div class="header">4. **Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS)**</div> <div class="text">Capturing carbon dioxide emissions from industrial processes and power plants, transporting it, and securely storing it underground to prevent its release into the atmosphere.</div> <div class="header">5. **Sustainable Agriculture**</div> <div class="text">Promoting agricultural practices that enhance carbon sequestration in soils, reduce methane emissions from livestock, and optimize land use.</div> <div class="header">6. **Waste Management**</div> <div class="text">Implementing efficient waste management practices, including recycling, composting, and reducing landfill methane emissions, to minimize greenhouse gas emissions from waste.</div> <div class="header">7. **Climate-Friendly Transportation**</div> <div class="text">Encouraging the use of electric vehicles, public transportation, and alternative fuels to reduce emissions from the transportation sector.</div> <div class="header">8. **International Cooperation**</div> <div class="text">Facilitating global collaboration to address climate change, including international agreements and commitments to reduce emissions collectively.</div> <div class="header">Difference Between Mitigation and Adaptation</div> <div class="text">Mitigation focuses on reducing or preventing the emission of greenhouse gases to limit climate change, while adaptation involves adjusting to the impacts of climate change.
