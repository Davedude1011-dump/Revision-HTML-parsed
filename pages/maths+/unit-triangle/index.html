In the unit triangle, cos θ = adjacent/hypotenuse. Therefore, cos θ = b/1 = b, where 'b' is the length of the adjacent side.</div> <div class="header">Tangent (tan θ)</div> <div class="text">Tangent is defined as the ratio of the length of the side opposite the angle to the side adjacent to the angle. In the unit triangle, tan θ = opposite/adjacent.
