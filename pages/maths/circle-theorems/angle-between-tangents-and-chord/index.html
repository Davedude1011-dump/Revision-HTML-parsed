Connect points \( A \) and \( P \) to form triangle \( APT \) and points \( B \) and \( P \) to form triangle \( BPT \).</div> <div class="text">3. Since \( PT \) is a tangent to the circle, \( \angle OTP \) is a right angle (tangent-radius theorem).</div> <div class="text">4.
