Respiratory Tract Defenses</div> <div class="text">- **Cough Reflex:** The respiratory tract is equipped with defense mechanisms like the cough reflex, which expels irritants and pathogens. Urogenital Tract Defenses</div> <div class="text">- **Urinary Tract Flushing:** The flow of urine helps to flush out pathogens from the urinary tract, preventing infections.</div> <div class="text">- **Vaginal Acidity:** The vagina maintains an acidic pH, creating an environment that discourages the growth of harmful bacteria and yeast.</div> <div class="header">6. Eye Defenses</div> <div class="text">- **Tears:** Tears contain enzymes and antibodies that help protect the eyes from infections.
