Clean Energy</div> <div class="text">- **Zero Emissions:** Fuel cells produce electricity with minimal to zero emissions of pollutants such as carbon dioxide (CO₂), nitrogen oxides (NOₓ), and particulate matter. Versatility</div> <div class="text">- **Diverse Applications:** Fuel cells have diverse applications across various sectors, including transportation (e.g., fuel cell vehicles), stationary power generation (e.g., backup power systems, distributed energy), and portable electronics (e.g., laptops, smartphones).</div> <div class="text">- **Scalability:** Fuel cell systems can be scaled up or down to meet different power requirements, from small, portable devices to large-scale power plants. Reliability and Durability</div> <div class="text">- **Long Lifespan:** Fuel cells exhibit high reliability and durability, with long operational lifespans compared to traditional batteries and combustion engines.
