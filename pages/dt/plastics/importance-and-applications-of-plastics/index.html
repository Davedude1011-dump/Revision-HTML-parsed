Applications of Plastics</div> <div class="text">- **Packaging:** Plastics are extensively used in packaging applications, including bottles, containers, films, and wraps, due to their versatility, lightweight, and barrier properties, ensuring product protection and shelf life.</div> <div class="text">- **Construction:** Plastics play a vital role in the construction industry for applications such as pipes, insulation, roofing materials, windows, doors, and composite building materials, offering durability, thermal insulation, and design flexibility.</div> <div class="text">- **Automotive:** Plastics are widely utilized in automotive manufacturing for components such as bumpers, interior trim, dashboard panels, seating, and lightweight structural parts, contributing to fuel efficiency and vehicle safety.</div> <div class="text">- **Electronics:** Plastics are integral to the electronics industry for housing components, insulation, circuit boards, displays, and packaging materials, providing electrical insulation, mechanical protection, and design aesthetics.</div> <div class="text">- **Medical:** Plastics are essential in the medical field for applications such as surgical instruments, implants, prosthetics, medical devices, packaging, and disposable items, offering biocompatibility, sterilizability, and cost-effectiveness.</div> <div class="text">- **Agriculture:** Plastics are used in agriculture for applications such as mulch films, greenhouse coverings, irrigation systems, crop protection, and packaging, enhancing crop yield, water efficiency, and pest control.</div> <div class="text">- **Consumer Goods:** Plastics are ubiquitous in consumer products such as toys, household appliances, furniture, sporting goods, and personal care items, offering design versatility, durability, and affordability.</div> <div class="header">3.
